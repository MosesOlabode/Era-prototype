How it works

Not posts.
Chapters.

Every meaningful thing belongs to an Era. The arc stays coherent.

01
Begin an Era

Name your chapter. Set visibility. Give it a cover. The Era is the container — everything else builds inside it.

02
Add entries

Post updates as the chapter unfolds. Milestones. Setbacks. Reflections. Proof. Let the arc form naturally over time.

03
Close the chapter

When that era ends, mark it complete. The full journey — start to finish — stays intact and searchable forever.
