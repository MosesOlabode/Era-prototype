Life is lived in
chapters.

Begin an Era. Build it over time. End it when that chapter is complete. Keep the record — private, public, or somewhere between.

How it works

Not posts.
Chapters.

Every meaningful thing you create belongs to an Era. The journey stays coherent.

01
Begin an Era

Name your chapter. Set the visibility. Give it a cover. The Era is the container — everything else builds inside it.

02
Drop traces

Add entries as the chapter unfolds. Milestones. Setbacks. Reflections. Proof. Let the arc form naturally over time.

03
Close the chapter

When that era ends, mark it complete. The full journey — beginning, buildup, and ending — stays intact forever.
